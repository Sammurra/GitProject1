Pasta Bolognesse Italian

Delicious pasta dishes from classic spaghetti Bolognese to lasagne and linguine.

Pasta is probably in the top 5 list of the most versatile dishes to make for any level of home cook. It fits the bill when cooked for a crowd or potluck, and makes an easy family meal or simple dinner for two.

And with pasta, you'll always have variety. It can be made creamy or saucy, vegetarian or with chicken, fish, meat and more. Cooking pasta can be an all day affair, a 5 minute homemade sauce, or store-bought in the jar, and taste amazing every time.

Ingregients

  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato paste

Steps

  1. Spray a slow cooker with nonstick cooking spray. Place chicken breasts in the slow cooker
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet until shimmering
  3. Add onion
  4. Add mushrooms and cook until onion is translucent and mushrooms are soft
